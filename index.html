What We Do

Four ways we show up.

We believe the early church model still works: a community that meets real needs, in person, until lives visibly change. Every project we run falls under one of these.

🏠

Restore Homes

Free emergency repairs, accessibility builds, and disaster response — for people who genuinely can't afford them.

❤️

Restore Lives

Recovery support, job readiness, and résumé and interview coaching for people rebuilding from the ground up.

🤝

Restore Families

Furniture, move-in help, baby supplies, and crisis meals for families starting over.

📖

Restore Dignity

Every volunteer and every family we serve contributes a skill, not just a need. No one is only a recipient.

About

A movement before it's an organization.

Christopher Osburn — Founder & Chapter Lead

Operation: Restore exists to prove, one town at a time, that lives change when a community meets real needs in person — and lets everyone, not just "helpers" and "the helped," contribute something. We do free skilled labor for people in genuine need, and we ask everyone we help what they're good at, because everyone has something to give back.

Based in Jackson, TN. Reach out by text to set up a service, ask about volunteering, or start a conversation about partnership.
